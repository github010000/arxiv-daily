## [2/10] Learning to Reason by Analogy via Retrieval-Augmented Reinforcement Fine-Tuning

- **저자**: Zilin Xiao, Qi Ma, Chun-cheng Jason Chen
- **공개일**: 2026-06-11
- **링크**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13680v1

**요약**

RA-RFT는 gold-relevance distillation 기반의 reasoning-aware retrieval와 retrieval-augmented reinforcement fine-tuning을 결합한 사후 학습 프레임워크입니다.
